Our Commitment to Privacy Arrow Executive Sales values and respects your privacy. We are committed to protecting personal information and complying with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and disclose personal information. 2. What Information We Collect • Personal details: Name, email address, phone number, job title, company name. • Recruitment-related information: CVs, work history, qualifications, references. • Sensitive information: Only with your consent (e.g., health or criminal checks for recruitment purposes). • Website and ad interactions: Data from LinkedIn Lead Gen forms, cookies, and analytics. 3. How We Collect Information • Directly from you via forms, emails, LinkedIn ads, or phone calls. • Through our recruitment & CRM platforms, which are secure, cloud-based systems. • From publicly available sources or third parties (with your consent). 4. How We Use Your Information • To provide recruitment and consulting services. • To communicate about job opportunities, resources, and events. • To improve our services and marketing campaigns. • To comply with legal obligations. 5. Data Storage and Security All data is stored securely in Loxo and Membrain, which use encryption and comply with global security standards (SOC 2, GDPR). We implement strict access controls and monitor compliance. Data in transit is protected using TLS encryption. 6. Disclosure of Information • With clients for recruitment purposes (with your consent). • With service providers who assist in our operations (under confidentiality agreements). • When required by law. 7. Your Rights • Access and correct your personal information. • Withdraw consent for marketing communications. • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations). 8. LinkedIn Ads Compliance • We collect only necessary details (e.g., name, email, job title). • Your data will be used solely for recruitment and consulting purposes. • By submitting your details, you consent to this use. 9.
