Our Commitment to Privacy Arrow Executive Sales values and respects your privacy. We comply with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). This policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and disclose personal information. ## 2. What Information We Collect - **Personal details:** Name, email address, phone number, job title, company name. - **Recruitment-related information:** CVs, work history, qualifications, references. - **Sensitive information:** Collected only with your consent (e.g., health or criminal checks). - **Website and ad interactions:** LinkedIn Lead Gen form data, cookies, analytics. ## 3. How We Collect Information - Directly from you via forms, emails, LinkedIn ads, or phone calls. - Through secure, cloud-based recruitment and CRM platforms. - From publicly available sources or third parties (with your consent). ## 4. How We Use Your Information - To provide recruitment and consulting services. - To communicate about job opportunities, resources, and events. - To improve services and marketing campaigns. - To meet legal and compliance obligations. ## 5. Data Storage and Security All data is securely stored in **Loxo** and **Membrain**, which use strong encryption and comply with global security standards (SOC 2, GDPR). We use strict access controls, monitor compliance, and protect data in transit with TLS encryption. ## 6. Disclosure of Information - With clients for recruitment purposes (with your consent). - With service providers assisting in our operations (subject to confidentiality). - When required by law. ## 7. Your Rights You may: - Access and correct your personal information. - Withdraw consent for marketing communications. - Request deletion of your data (where legally possible). ## 8. LinkedIn Ads Compliance - We collect only essential details (e.g., name, email, job title). - Your data is used solely for recruitment and consulting. - By submitting your details, you consent to this use. ## 9.
