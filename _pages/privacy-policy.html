Arrow Executive Sales Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 10th December, 2025

1. Our Commitment to Privacy

Arrow Executive Sales values and respects your privacy. We comply with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). This policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and disclose personal information.

2. What Information We Collect

  • Personal details: Name, email address, phone number, job title, company name.
  • Recruitment-related information: CVs, work history, qualifications, references.
  • Sensitive information: Collected only with your consent (e.g., health or criminal checks).
  • Website and ad interactions: LinkedIn Lead Gen form data, cookies, analytics.

3. How We Collect Information

  • Directly from you via forms, emails, LinkedIn ads, or phone calls.
  • Through secure, cloud-based recruitment and CRM platforms.
  • From publicly available sources or third parties (with your consent).

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide recruitment and consulting services.
  • To communicate about job opportunities, resources, and events.
  • To improve services and marketing campaigns.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

5. Data Storage and Security

All data is securely stored in Loxo and Membrain, which use encryption and comply with global security standards (SOC 2, GDPR). We implement strict access controls, monitor compliance, and protect data in transit with TLS encryption.

6. Disclosure of Information

  • With clients for recruitment purposes (with your consent).
  • With service providers assisting in our operations (under confidentiality).
  • When required by law.

7. Your Rights

  • Access and correct your personal information.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications.
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations).

8. LinkedIn Ads Compliance

  • We collect only essential details (e.g., name, email, job title).
  • Your data will be used solely for recruitment and consulting purposes.
  • By submitting your details, you consent to this use.
