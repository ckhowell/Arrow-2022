How does a Sales Candidate Assessment work?

1

Select the sales position you need to fill

2

Customise and configure to suit your needs

3

Identify your success criteria for each role

4

Post your job description to appropriate job sites

5

Candidates receive and complete assessment link

6

Get the results including recommendations

7

Look at recommended candidate resumes, interview suitable ones and use assessment questions to confirm ability

8

Make the offer, and complete reference checks and do your diligence
