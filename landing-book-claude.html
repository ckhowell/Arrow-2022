Meet Jason Howes

Author of Crystal Ball Recruiting and Australia's top sales hiring specialist

Jason Howes is the founder of Arrow Executive Sales and one of Australia's leading specialists in sales recruitment and performance consulting. With over 35 years in the field, he's seen what happens when businesses rely on luck to find talent… and how powerful it becomes when they stop.

Jason has partnered with Objective Management Group (OMG) to bring science-based recruitment to the Australian market. His clients include national organisations, growth-focused SMEs, and global teams looking to improve the way they attract and manage sales talent.

Crystal Ball Recruiting distils what he's learned through hundreds of hires and consulting engagements: how to identify the traits that matter, how to measure them, and how to build a system around them.
