The 10-Step Sales Hiring Operating System

Every chapter of Crystal Ball Recruiting tackles one part of the sales hiring process, from evaluating your existing team to training the people who make hiring decisions.

1. Assessing Your Current Team

Identify the real capabilities of your sales team and pinpoint the gaps that affect performance. Learn to separate ability from activity.

2. Making an ROI on Salespeople

Understand the financial impact of every hire. Use the included recruitment cost breakdown to calculate the return on each salesperson.

3. Defining the Role Clearly

Learn how to create precise role definitions and integrate CRM automation to keep expectations consistent and measurable.

4. Compensation & Incentives

Build packages that attract achievers. Balance extrinsic motivators (money) with intrinsic drivers (purpose, recognition).

5. Attracting Top Sales Talent

Write ads that cut through to the top 20% (who aren't looking). Learn the language that elite performers respond to.

6. Removing Bias with Science

Replace "gut feel" with the STAR™ assessment process to evaluate fit objectively, clinically, and consistently.

7. Selecting the Best Players

Build a repeatable interview process that reveals actual sales behavior, rather than just how well they perform in the interview room.

8. Onboarding for Success

Turn new hires into productive contributors faster with an ACTIONABLE 90-day plan that drives early wins.

9. Retaining Top Performers

Learn how to build loyalty and motivation through structure, accountability, and opportunity… not just pay rises.

10. Training Hiring Managers

Equip your team leaders to make better decisions through the STAR™ Workshop—turning hiring into a measurable skill.
